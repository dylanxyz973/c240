Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main purpose of WallBreak Lumi?

WallBreak Lumi was created to support individuals who may be struggling emotionally, feeling socially isolated, or hesitant to reach out to others. Our goal is to offer a gentle stepping stone for those ready to build confidence, practice interactions, and gradually re-engage with the world around them.

How can the chatbot help me practice everyday conversations?

Lumi acts as a safe, completely judgment-free space. It can suggest engaging conversation starters, simulate daily social scenarios, and help you practice expressing your ideas so you feel much more comfortable and natural when talking to others in real life.

Can the chatbot help me find people with similar interests?

Yes! Beyond chatting, Lumi can guide you toward discovering activities, topics, and communities that align with your personal interests, making it easier to take those first comfortable steps toward forming meaningful real-world connections.

Is my emotional support data kept private?

Absolutely. Your emotional well-being and trust are our top priorities. All interactive chat histories are encrypted and securely stored under your private profile, and we strictly ensure your data is never shared or sold to third parties.
