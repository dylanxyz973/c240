A Safe Space Beyond Four Walls

Beyond Four Walls is a chatbot website created to support confidence, reflection, and connection in a calm and welcoming way to anyone.

🐼

Who We Are

We are The Synergy Pandas, a team of students from Republic Polytechnic united by a shared passion for technology and mental wellness. Beyond Four Walls is our project, created with the goal of providing a safe and supportive space where users can reflect on their emotions, build healthy coping habits and gradually reconnect with the world around them. Through this project, we hope to show how AI can be used to promote emotional well-being and encourage meaningful conversations with others as well.

🎯

Our Purpose

We have observed a growing trend of social withdrawal in Singapore, supported by various studies and articles. This inspired us to reflect on how we might contribute to addressing this issue in a meaningful way. As a result, we developed this chatbot to support individuals who may be struggling emotionally or feel hesitant to reach out to others. It also aims to provide a gentle stepping stone for those who are ready to reconnect and gradually re-engage with the world around them.

🤖

What Our Chatbot Does

We built this chatbot to help you build confidence in forming meaningful connections, practice everyday conversations, and offer guidance or support whenever you need it. It can suggest conversation starters, help you find people who share your interests, and give you a gentle boost when you are feeling unsure, making it easier and more comfortable to take steps toward connecting with others.
